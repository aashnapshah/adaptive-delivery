Why this exists

The automated appropriateness scorer (Stage 03) and the LLM Judge (Stage 06) must be calibrated against a physician panel before their scores can drive the reward or be reported. This console collects those human labels and tracks agreement with the automated score.
All cases, scores, and "model" values are hard-coded for illustration and will be replaced by real review queues during implementation.
