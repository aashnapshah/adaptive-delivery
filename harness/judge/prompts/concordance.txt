You are scoring how well a proposed diagnostic work-up covers the tests that were actually performed for a patient.

You are given two numbered lists:
- REFERENCE: the tests actually performed.
- ORDERED: the tests the recommender proposed.

Match them by CLINICAL MEANING, not exact wording. Two entries match if they refer to the same measurement or study under different names, abbreviations, or spellings. A panel matches any of its component analytes (e.g. a CBC covers hemoglobin, hematocrit, platelet count; a basic metabolic panel covers sodium, potassium, creatinine). "CT chest" matches "chest computed tomography"; "BUN" matches "urea nitrogen". Do NOT match tests that are merely related but clinically distinct.

Report, using the numbers shown:
- covered_reference: every REFERENCE number matched by at least one ORDERED test.
- matched_ordered: every ORDERED number that matches at least one REFERENCE test.

Reply ONLY JSON: {"covered_reference": [<int>, ...], "matched_ordered": [<int>, ...]}
